Blueberry Pie

  • Description
  • Ingredients

Description

This is a top secret class A level 5 clearence blueberry pie recipe that has been kept secret among a generation of Lim family members.

Ingredients

Portion size: 1 dangerously delicious pie

  • 1 9" deep dish pie crust

Filling

  • 1 pound of the finest blueberries
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 3 eggs

Topping

  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 3/4 cup flour
  • 6 tbsp. butter

Directions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Place blueberries in pie crust.
  3. Mix filling ingredients in a bowl and pour over berries.
  4. Mix topping ingredients thoroughly and crumble over top of pie.
  5. Place in oven for 1.5 hours
